About Our Club

Learn, trade, and compete in a supportive environment designed for business minded math and coding enthusiasts. We focus on utilizing traditional fundamental and technical analysis along with quantitative methods to trade effectively.

Unparalleled Quantitative Trading Opportunity at LHS

The Lexington High School Paper Trading Club offers a unique opportunity to explore financial markets using a quantitative approach. We combine mathematical concepts with technical analysis to help students develop practical trading skills.

Our club provides a supportive and engaging environment where students can learn about trading markets, develop coding skills, and participate in exciting paper trading competitions – all without the financial risk.
