Quantitative Trading at LHS

The Lexington High School Paper Trading Club offers a unique opportunity to explore financial markets using a quantitative approach. We combine mathematical concepts with technical analysis to help students develop practical trading skills.

Our club provides a supportive and engaging environment where students can learn about trading markets, develop coding skills, and participate in exciting paper trading competitions ‚Äì all without the financial risk.
